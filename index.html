Vision Rijal
student
After the world suffered from utter chaos following the unification of the 7 realms by the Elders, many lost the will to live, existence itself was dreadful. But this dread did not come from the destruction and cleansing that followed the unification rather from the instinct to refuse change deep inside every sentient being, to eat and drink with the same monsters that slayed your brethren and to see them eye to eye was something impossible. After his revival ; Vision, fed up by the atrocities of the Elders who claimed themselves messengers of God, set out to end misery and suffering once and for all whatever it cost.
It is said the dragon slayer, bringer of chaos got his title following his revival at the altar of solitude, soon after his revival the world crumbled and burnt, while many considered the revival of Vision to be a bad omen the heavens had something different reserved in fate, the dragon slayer was reborn after being slain by one of the Elder but this time in his true meta-physical body.
